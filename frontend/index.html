How It Works

1. Report

Snap a picture of the civic issue, select a category (e.g., Pothole, Water Leakage), write a short description, and add the location.

2. Track

Monitor the status of your reported issues in real-time. Watch them transition from "Pending" to "In Progress" and finally "Resolved".

3. Resolve

Admins assign maintenance personnel to resolve issues and update the status on the portal, keeping the community clean and safe.
